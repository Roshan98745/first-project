My schooling

My educational path has been characterized by multiple changes, since I attended multiple schools until eighth grade. I was resolved to make the most of every experience, even though these frequent moves presented challenges. I was able to concentrate on my studies and academic development at Viswa Niketan Secondary School from grades 8 through 12, where I also discovered stability and a sense of belonging. I continuously received a B+ on all board exams during these years, proving my excellent work ethic and commitment to learning.

I've always been naturally curious and eager to learn. I was and am willing to learn something new every day and excited to investigate new topics. Even in the face of difficulties, my passion for studying kept me focused on my studies. Education, in my opinion, is about accepting new ideas and broadening one's perspective on the world, not only about getting good grades. I was able to cultivate these ideals during my time at Viswa Niketan Secondary School, which also encouraged me to keep learning and developing.
